Special Shakshuka

Description

The word shakshuka comes from Arabic, meaning, “a haphazard mixture” or “all mixed up.”

Shakshuka features poached eggs in a hearty, spiced tomato and pepper sauce. Some people like to top it with tangy feta cheese and fresh cilantro or parsley.

Ingredients

  • 50 g avocado, peeled and sliced
  • 150 g canned tomatoes
  • 115 g red bell pepper, chopped
  • 100 g eggs (2 large)
  • 5 g garlic, peeled and minced
  • 25 g parsley
  • 35 g whole grain bread

Optional:Add a pinch of sea salt, black pepper and cumin.

Steps

  1. Preheat the pan and add canned tomatoes, along with the chopped bell pepper. Cook for about 10 minutes on medium heat. Season with salt, pepper, cumin, or your favourite spice mix.
  2. Add garlic and stir.
  3. Crack the eggs into the tomatoes and season them as desired. Cook the eggs for about 7-10 minutes, making sure that the egg white is cooked through, and the egg yolk is still runny.
  4. Sprinkle with freshly chopped parsley leaves and top with avocado, serve hot with a slice of toasted bread YUM!
