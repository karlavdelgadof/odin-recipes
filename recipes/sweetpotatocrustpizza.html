Sweet Potato Crust Pizza

Description

A brilliant idea if you're craving some pizza but don't want to drop off the healthy lifestyle you've been up to.

This sweet potato pizza crust is surprisingly tasty and really easy to make. It’s made with just 6 wholesome ingredients: Cooked sweet potatoes, oat flour,egg, tomato sauce, low-fat mozarella and commonly used species (salt, pepper, oregano, fresh basil leaves, garlic powder).

Ingredients

  • 1 medium sweet potato, peeled & cooked
  • 80g oats or oat flour
  • 1 egg
  • 60g tomato sauce
  • 50g low-fat mozzarella or plant-based cheese
  • salt, pepper, oregano, fresh basil leaves, garlic powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200ºC or 400ºF.
  2. Pulse the sweet potato and oats through the food processor.
  3. Add the egg and optional garlic powder, salt and pepper and mix again.
  4. Line a tray with baking paper. Press the mixture with your hands shaping it into a circle.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, until the edges of the crust start to turn brown.
  6. Remove from the oven, allow to cool and carefully flip the crust on another baking sheet so the dry side faces down. Bake for another 5-10 minutes.
  7. Remove from the oven again, spread tomato sauce and mozzarella on top and pop back into the oven to melt the cheese.

Optional: Add oregano and/or basil leaves once you take it out from the oven and ENJOY!
